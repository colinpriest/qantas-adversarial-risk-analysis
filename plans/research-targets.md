# Practical “research targets” list (for your next step)

If you want to justify these assumptions with data, the most valuable datasets/literature to look for are:

1. **Two-strikes consequences quantification**
  - spill frequency after second strike in Australia
  - subsequent board turnover / CEO turnover rates
  - market CAR around second-strike + spill outcomes
2. **Switching/backtracking penalties**
  - CAR and vote impacts when firms change governance response between announcement and AGM
  - ASA recommendation changes after “weakening” governance commitments
3. **CEO departure mode statistics**
  - resign vs sacked frequencies conditional on crisis severity
  - market reaction distribution (mean + volatility) by mode
4. **Deference / governance quality proxies**
  - board independence measures predicting likelihood of forced termination vs “retirement”
  - governance reviews linking deference language to observed outcomes
